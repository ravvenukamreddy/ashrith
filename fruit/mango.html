Mango

(Mangifera indica)

Mango is a fragrant tropical fruit known for its rich sweetness and buttery texture. Loved by kids and adults alike, it brings bright color and natural energy to the plate— perfect for a sunny school market!

Season: Summer Best: May–June Origin: South Asia

How to Eat / Serve

  • Wash, peel, and slice into cubes for bowls or lunchboxes.
  • Blend into smoothies or lassi for a cool treat.
  • Top yogurt, oats, or salads for natural sweetness.

Medicinal Values

  • Rich in antioxidants (beta-carotene) that support eye health and immunity.
  • Contains digestive enzymes (amylases) that may aid gentle digestion.
  • Traditional use in soothing mild inflammation and boosting vitality.

Health Benefits

  • High in vitamin C — supports immune function and skin health.
  • Good source of dietary fiber — helps maintain a healthy gut.
  • Natural energy booster — a kid-friendly way to increase fruit intake.
