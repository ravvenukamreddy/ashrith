Sweet Potato (Chilakada Dumpa / Sakkarai Valli Kizhangu)

(Ipomoea batatas)

A natural energy giver rooted in South Indian tradition — warm, filling, and excellent for winter and evening snacks.

Season: Winter Best: Dec–Feb Origin: Tropical Americas

How to Eat

  • Roast or boil and serve with a sprinkle of salt.
  • Make sweet potato porridge.
  • Add to curries or cutlets.

Medicinal Values

  • Rich in beta-carotene (good for eyes).
  • Provides slow-release energy.
  • Supports gut health and digestion.

Health Benefits

  • Keeps body warm in winter.
  • Boosts immunity and stamina.
  • Kid-friendly, natural sweet snack.
