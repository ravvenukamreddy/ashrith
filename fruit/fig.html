Fig (Anjeer / Athi)

(Ficus carica)

Soft, seedy, and naturally sweet. Figs restore warmth and support digestion during winter months.

Season: Winter Also: Late Summer Origin: Mediterranean & India

How to Eat / Serve

  • Eat fresh halves with a drizzle of honey.
  • Chop into salads, oats, or curd bowls.
  • Simmer dried figs in milk for a winter dessert.

Medicinal Values

  • Soluble fiber supports gut movement.
  • Minerals (calcium, magnesium) support bone health.
  • Antioxidants aid circulation and skin glow.

Health Benefits

  • Warms and nourishes during cold months.
  • Gently relieves constipation.
  • Natural sweetener alternative for kids.
