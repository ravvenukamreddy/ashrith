Fenugreek (Methi / Menthi)

(Trigonella foenum-graecum)

A staple seed in Indian kitchens—slightly bitter but extremely powerful for digestion, immunity, and winter warmth.

Season: Winter Also: Early Summer Origin: Mediterranean–India

How to Use

  • Soak overnight and eat in the morning for digestion.
  • Add to rasam, sambar, pickles.
  • Sprout fenugreek for salads.

Medicinal Values

  • Controls blood sugar naturally.
  • Great for hair, skin, and digestion.
  • Reduces inflammation.

Health Benefits

  • Helps regulate metabolism in winter.
  • Boosts immunity and energy.
  • Supports gut health.
