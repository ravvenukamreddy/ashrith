Wood Apple (Bael / Vilvam)

(Aegle marmelos)

A classic summer fruit in traditional homes. Wood apple pulp is cooling and digestive—perfect when heat and tummy upsets are common.

Season: Summer Best: March–June Origin: Indian subcontinent

How to Eat / Serve

  • Crack the shell, scoop pulp, strain with water—add jaggery & a pinch of cardamom for bael sherbet.
  • Mix pulp with curd for a soothing summer drink.
  • Use in chutneys or sweet preserves.

Medicinal Values

  • Traditionally used for diarrhea & IBS; supports gut lining.
  • Naturally cooling; balances heat-related irritation.
  • Aids gentle detox and digestion.

Health Benefits

  • Hydrating summer cooler for the stomach.
  • Fiber supports regularity and gut comfort.
  • Aromatic polyphenols aid overall wellness.
