Ice Apple

(Borassus flabellifer)

Also known as Nungu or Tadgola, Ice Apple is a translucent, jelly-like fruit from the palmyra palm. Naturally sweet and wonderfully cooling, it’s a summer favorite across South and Southeast Asia.

Season: Summer Best: April–June Origin: South & Southeast Asia

How to Eat / Serve

  • Chill and enjoy the peeled segments as a light snack.
  • Slice into fruit bowls or tender-coconut–style desserts.
  • Blend with milk/water, a pinch of cardamom, and jaggery for a summer drink.

Medicinal Values

  • Naturally hydrating with fluids and electrolytes — a traditional “cooling” fruit.
  • Light, gentle-on-the-stomach snack often preferred in hot weather.
  • Provides small amounts of vitamins and minerals with minimal heaviness.

Health Benefits

  • Helps rehydrate and beat the heat during peak summer.
  • Low in calories — a refreshing alternative to heavy sweets.
  • Soft texture makes it easy for kids and elders to eat.
