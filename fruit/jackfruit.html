Jackfruit

(Artocarpus heterophyllus)

South India’s giant! Young fruit is a hearty veggie; ripe bulbs are honey-sweet and energizing—great just before peak summer.

Season: March–June Peak: Early Summer Origin: Western Ghats

How to Eat / Serve

  • Ripe: eat bulbs plain or add to fruit bowls.
  • Raw: cook as curry/stir-fry; also used as veg “meat.”
  • Boil & roast seeds for snacks or gravies.

Medicinal Values

  • Potassium & fiber support heart and gut.
  • Vitamin B & minerals provide slow energy.
  • Polyphenols support antioxidant defense.

Health Benefits

  • Strength-building fruit for pre-summer months.
  • Helps maintain fullness and energy.
  • Seeds add protein, iron, and fiber.
