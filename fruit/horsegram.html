Horse Gram (Kulthi / Ulavalu)

(Macrotyloma uniflorum)

A traditional South Indian superfood rich in protein and heat-producing energy. Commonly used in rainy and winter seasons for strength.

Season: Winter Also: Monsoon Origin: South India

How to Eat / Serve

  • Horse gram rasam (ulava charu) served with rice or idiyappam.
  • Sprouted horse gram salads and curries.
  • Powder roasted horse gram for health drinks.

Medicinal Values

  • Traditionally used for kidney stone relief.
  • Improves digestion and metabolism.
  • Rich in iron, protein and antioxidants.

Health Benefits

  • Gives warmth and energy in cold seasons.
  • Supports weight management.
  • Strengthens muscles & bones.
