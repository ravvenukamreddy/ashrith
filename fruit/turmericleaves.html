Turmeric Leaves (Haldi Patra / Pasupu Aaku)

(Curcuma longa)

Aromatic leaves used for steaming festive sweets and herbal remedies. Their natural oils add fragrance and health benefits to food.

Season: Monsoon Best: July–October Origin: India

How to Use

  • Steam sweets like patoli/holige inside the leaf.
  • Add to herbal kashayams for cold & cough.
  • Wrap fish or veggies and steam to infuse aroma.

Medicinal Values

  • Natural anti-inflammatory properties.
  • Aromatic oils help clear nasal passages.
  • Strengthens immunity during monsoon.

Health Benefits

  • Helps reduce cold & congestion.
  • Improves digestive fire.
  • Enhances nutrient absorption when steamed.
