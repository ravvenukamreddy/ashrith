Jamun (Indian Blackberry)

(Syzygium cumini)

Deep purple, tangy-sweet, and stainy fingers! Jamun is a summer–monsoon favorite in South India, linked with blood-sugar balance.

Season: May–July Also: Early Monsoon Origin: India & SE Asia

How to Eat / Serve

  • Wash and eat plain with a pinch of salt.
  • Make jamun lemonade or add to fruit salads.
  • Use pulp in ice pops or smoothies.

Medicinal Values

  • Traditionally associated with blood-sugar regulation.
  • Antioxidant-rich anthocyanins support heart health.
  • Astringent; supports oral and gum health.

Health Benefits

  • Summer fruit that counters heat and fatigue.
  • Supports healthy metabolism during humid months.
  • Vitamin C & iron help maintain energy.
