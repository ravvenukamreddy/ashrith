Curry Leaves (Karuveppilai / Karivepaku)

(Murraya koenigii)

A humble kitchen hero — rich in iron, antioxidants, and aroma. Curry leaves support digestion, hair health, and overall immunity.

Season: Year-round Peak: Post-monsoon Origin: India & Sri Lanka

How to Use

  • Temper in dals, rice, rasam, curries.
  • Make curry leaf powder (karivepaku podi).
  • Blend into chutneys for added nutrition.

Medicinal Values

  • Supports hair growth (rich in iron).
  • Improves digestion and gut health.
  • Used in Ayurveda for managing cholesterol.

Health Benefits

  • Boosts immunity and metabolism.
  • Great for skin and hair nourishment.
  • Natural detoxifier.
