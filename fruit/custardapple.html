Custard Apple (Sitaphal)

(Annona squamosa)

Creamy-sweet segments in a knobbly shell. A gentle, nourishing fruit for late monsoon to winter.

Season: Aug–Nov Peak: Early Winter Origin: Tropics

How to Eat / Serve

  • Chill, split, and scoop with a spoon (avoid seeds).
  • Blend pulp into milkshakes/ice cream.
  • Fold into yogurt or kheer for desserts.

Medicinal Values

  • Magnesium & potassium—muscle and heart support.
  • Gentle on digestion; soothing after humid weather.
  • Antioxidants for tissue repair.

Health Benefits

  • Comforting energy for cooler days.
  • Supports recovery from monsoon fatigue.
  • Kid-friendly sweetness without added sugar.
