Drumstick (Moringa Pods)

(Moringa oleifera)

A powerful vegetable pod famous in sambar and curries — known as a “miracle tree” for its rich minerals, vitamins and healing benefits.

Season: Year-round Best: Jan–June Origin: India

How to Eat

  • Add to sambar, pappu, or curries.
  • Steam and scrape pulp to mix with rice.
  • Use pods + leaves for combined nutrition.

Medicinal Values

  • Rich in iron, calcium and vitamin C.
  • Strengthens bones and immunity.
  • Good for digestion and cleansing.

Health Benefits

  • Boosts energy naturally.
  • Promotes healthy skin and hair.
  • Excellent for growing children.
